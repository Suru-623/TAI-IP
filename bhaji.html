Pavbhaji Recipe



Certainly! Here's a simple and delicious recipe for Pav Bhaji along with the ingredients you'll need:

    Ingredients:
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (potatoes, cauliflower, peas, carrots), chopped
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 1 bell pepper (capsicum), finely chopped
  • 2 large tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup green peas
  • 4 tablespoons pav bhaji masala
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • Lemon wedges
      • Instructions:

    Boil the chopped mixed vegetables and green peas until they are soft. Drain and set aside. In a large pan or kadai, heat oil and butter together. Add finely chopped onions and sauté until they turn translucent. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute until the raw smell disappears. Add chopped bell peppers and sauté for a couple of minutes. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they are soft and the oil starts to separate. Mash the boiled vegetables and add them to the pan. Mix well. Add pav bhaji masala, red chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Mix and mash the vegetables further. Add water as needed to achieve the desired consistency. Simmer the bhaji on low heat for 10-15 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld. In the meantime, slit the pav (buns) and toast them on a griddle with butter until they are golden brown. Garnish the bhaji with fresh coriander leaves. Serve the hot and spicy bhaji with buttered pav, chopped onions, and lemon wedges on the side. Enjoy your delicious Pav Bhaji!
