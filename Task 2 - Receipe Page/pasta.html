Pasta Recipe



Certainly! Here's a simple and delicious recipe for Pasta along with the ingredients you'll need:

    Ingredients:
  • 1 cup penne or any pasta of your choice
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili flakes (adjust to taste)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon sugar (optional, to balance acidity)
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves, chopped (for garnish)
      • Instructions:

    Cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside. In a large skillet or saucepan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped onions and sauté until they become translucent. Add minced garlic and sauté for another 1-2 minutes until fragrant. Pour in the crushed tomatoes and stir well. Add dried oregano, dried basil, red chili flakes, salt, and black pepper. Stir to combine. If the tomato sauce is too acidic, you can add a teaspoon of sugar to balance the flavors. Taste and adjust the seasonings as needed. Simmer the sauce on low heat for about 15-20 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld and the sauce to thicken. Once the sauce is ready, add the cooked pasta to the skillet and toss until the pasta is well-coated with the sauce. Garnish with fresh basil leaves. Serve the red sauce pasta hot, optionally topped with grated Parmesan cheese. Enjoy your homemade red sauce pasta!
