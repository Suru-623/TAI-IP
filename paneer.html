Palak Paneer Recipe



Certainly! Here's a simple and delicious recipe for Palak paneer along with the ingredients you'll need:

    Ingredients:
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (potatoes, cauliflower, peas, carrots), chopped
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 1 bell pepper (capsicum), finely chopped
  • 2 large tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup green peas
  • 1 kg palak
  • 1 kg paneer
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • Lemon wedges
      • Instructions:

    Blanch the Spinach: Boil water in a large pot. Add a pinch of salt. Add chopped spinach to the boiling water and blanch for 2-3 minutes. Drain the spinach and immediately transfer it to ice-cold water. Once cooled, blend the spinach into a smooth puree. Prepare Paneer: Heat a little oil in a pan and lightly fry the paneer cubes until they are golden brown. Set aside. Make Palak Paneer: In the same pan, add oil or ghee. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter. Add chopped onions and sauté until they become golden brown. Add Aromatics: Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies. Sauté for 1-2 minutes until the raw smell disappears. Add Tomatoes: Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they become soft and the oil starts to separate. Add Spices: Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, and salt. Mix well. Add Spinach Puree: Add the spinach puree and mix everything together. Cook for 5-7 minutes on medium heat. Add Paneer: Add the fried paneer cubes to the spinach mixture. Mix well and let it simmer for another 5-7 minutes. Finish with Garam Masala and Butter: Add garam masala and butter (if using). Stir and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes. Garnish and Serve: Optional: Garnish with a drizzle of cream. Serve hot with roti, naan, or rice. Enjoy your homemade Palak Paneer!
